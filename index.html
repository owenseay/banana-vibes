Banana Vibes

A banana painted with watercolors that accidentally transcended into something else entirely.

Scroll

One night I got bored and painted a banana with watercolors at my kitchen table.

I peeled a off a real banana and stuck it on the painting then I cut the banana out of the watercolor paper that I painted it on and taped it to my refrigerator where it has lived every since.

Then one night I got bored again and decided to feed my banana into an LLM to see what would happen.

The results were astonishing…

What started as an innocent brush stroke at my kitchen table suddenly multiplied into exponential banana chaos.

Hundreds of bananas—spinning, jittering, stretching, wobbling, leaping through the air.

Bananas that undulate, oscillate, pulsate and squish with sine-based scaling.

It was as though I had slipped on a proverbial banana peel and stumbled into some sort of an alternate banana realm.

Time faded into hours long conversation about trick logic, event triggers, bounce physics, elastic collisions, friction damping, gravity and lateral drift simulation.

My god! Some bananas even became hero jumpers with boosted super-arc acrobatic energy that have a 3% chance per frame of launching across the screen based on their cooldown state.

These are the banana vibes...
